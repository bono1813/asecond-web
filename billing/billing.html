Billing & Subscription Help

Effective date: 2026-02-15
Support: support@asecond.app

1. Taxes (VAT / Sales Tax)

Prices shown on the Pricing page exclude applicable taxes. VAT or sales tax may be added at checkout based on your location.

2. Receipts & Invoices

Receipts and tax handling are issued and managed by our Merchant of Record (payment provider). If you need billing support, email us with your purchase email.

3. Cancel subscription

You can cancel anytime from the checkout provider’s subscription portal.

If you cannot access the portal, you can request cancellation by email:

  • Send from the same email you used at checkout.
  • Subject: Cancel subscription
  • Include: your plan name (Plus/Premium) and the purchase email/receipt details if available.

Contact: support@asecond.app

4. Plan access

After purchase, your plan access is activated based on your subscription status. If access does not update, sign out/in or contact support.

5. Refund help

See Refund Policy. For billing issues, contact support@asecond.app.
