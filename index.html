FAQ

Clear and simple.

Is ASECOND a summary tool?

No. ASECOND is built to find precise moments inside videos and jump to them instantly. Summaries may be included in higher plans, but the core is precision search.

Will this work on every video?

Coverage depends on available text sources and platform conditions. ASECOND is designed to be robust, but results may vary by video.

Is it a monthly subscription?

Yes. Plans are billed monthly and can be cancelled anytime.
